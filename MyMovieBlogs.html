My favourite Animals

ANIMALS

Tiger

Tiger voice 🎼


Tigers are the largest big cats, known for their orange fur with black stripes, and are powerful, solitary hunters found in Asia. They are excellent sprinters and swimmers, and their unique stripe patterns provide camouflage and serve as individual identifiers. As apex predators, they play a crucial role in maintaining their ecosystems.

Cat

Cat voice 🎼


Cats, or Felis catus, are small domesticated carnivorous mammals that have lived with humans for thousands of years. They are known for being agile, independent, and curious, with a variety of breeds existing worldwide. Cats are carnivores and have a flexible body, retractable claws, sharp hearing, excellent night vision, and whiskers that help them navigate and sense their surroundings.
